## Findings
- **Pricing Tiers**:
  - Previously: Pro plan is $20/month, Ultra plan was $250/month.
  - New options introduced on May 20, 2026:
    - New $100/month Google AI Ultra plan (for lighter but regular dev needs, 5X tokens compared to $20/mo Pro plan).
    - Reduced price of the top-tier AI Ultra plan from $250 to $200/month (has a rate limit of 20X tokens compared to $20/mo Pro plan).
- **Token Pools / Quotas**:
  - Shared pool of tokens instead of separate independent rate limits (e.g., Gemini Pro, 8X tokens on Gemini Flash, or linear combination).
  - Standardized multipliers:
    - $100/mo Ultra = 5X Pro tokens
    - $200/mo Ultra = 20X Pro tokens
  - AI Credits: removing base AI credits to simplify subscriptions.
- **Varun Mohan & The 3x Quota Boost**:
  - Varun Mohan (former creator of Windsurf, now Director/lead of Antigravity at Google after Google's $2.4B acquisition).
  - Following developer backlash regarding the Antigravity 2.0 quotas and performance, Varun Mohan announced on X (formerly Twitter) that Google is tripling (3x) the usage limits for Gemini models across all paid tiers in Antigravity.
  - This 3x increase applies to the weekly quota limits and is permanently extended to address user complaints.
  - **Details on the 2-step boost**:
    - **Step 1 (Wednesday, May 20, 2026)**: Tripled the short-term rate limits and reset the weekly quotas for all users.
    - **Step 2 (Thursday, May 21, 2026)**: Tripled the weekly quota limits as well.
    - **Permanence**: Both Varun Mohan and Logan Kilpatrick confirmed that the 3x increase is permanent.
